Community Health services

Investing in community health services for deserved populations is an investment in Healthy ageing, as these services can help individuals stay active, engaged, and independent as they age, promoting overall wellness and quality of life.
  • To promote healthy aging through community health services and public health activities.
  • To provide accessible and affordable healthcare services to the underserved and marginalized communities.
  • To conduct medical camps in remote, coastal and rural areas to reach out to those who do not have access to healthcare facilities.
  • To screen for breast cancer and other non-communicable diseases in order to detect and treat them at an early stage.
  • To raise awareness about health issues and promote healthy lifestyle choices through awareness sessions and campaigns.
  • To conduct dementia screening for elders in the community to detect and manage cognitive decline.
  • To provide nutritional support and guidance to individuals and families as part of public health activities, in order to promote overall health and well-being.
Objectives

Healthy ageing is not just about living longer, but living well. Community health services play a crucial role in supporting underserved populations to age in a healthy and fulfilling way, promoting overall well-being and quality of life.
