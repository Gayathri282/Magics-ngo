Consultancy services

We offer consultancy services for Government or Non-government projects in various fields such as Age-friendly communities, Public Health, Geriatrics, Community Development, and Healthy Aging

Our team of experts can provide guidance, support, and expertise to help organizations and communities meet their goals and objectives in these areas. Whether you need assistance with program development, policy analysis, strategic planning, or evaluation, we can tailor our services to meet your specific needs.

Objectives

  • Enhancing program effectiveness: Collaborate with NGOs to enhance the effectiveness of their programs in promoting age-friendly communities, public health initiatives, geriatric services, community development, and healthy aging through expert guidance and support.
  • Facilitating policy development: Assist NGOs in developing and analyzing policies that align with best practices in promoting age-friendly communities, public health, geriatrics, community development, and healthy aging to ensure impactful and sustainable outcomes.
  • Supporting strategic planning: Work closely with NGOs to develop strategic plans that drive progress towards their goals in creating age-friendly environments and zero tolerance to elder abuse.
  • Providing comprehensive evaluation: Conduct thorough evaluations of NGO projects in age-friendly communities, public health, geriatrics, community development, and healthy aging to assess impact, measure success, and identify areas for improvement, ensuring the delivery of high-quality services to communities and organizations.

Our Services

  • Consultancy center for Age-Friendly cities and communities:would provide expertise and resources to assist local governments, organizations, and community members in creating environments that are inclusive and accessible for people of all ages. Services offered by such a consultancy center could include:
    1. Development of action plans and strategies for improving age-friendliness
    2. Assessments and evaluations of the current age-friendliness of a city or community
    3. Workshops and training sessions on age-friendly design principles and practices
    4. Collaboration with local stakeholders to identify and address specific needs and challenges
      5. Research and data analysis to inform decision-making and policy development
  • Internship Facility and Volunteership : We offer internship opportunities for students and professionals interested in age-friendly initiatives. Interns gain hands-on experience in conducting research, program evaluation, and community engagement. Thrilled to see students from multidisciplinary backgrounds coming together for internships from different parts of India. It is inspiring to witness the diversity of fields ranging from Public Health, social work, Law, B.com, Advertisement, Communication and Design, Psychology and Management studies, among others.
  • Training Centre: We provide training programs for stakeholders, including local government officials, healthcare providers, social workers, and community members. These training sessions cover topics such as age-friendly design, elder abuse prevention, and caregiver support.
  • Resource Centre for Elder Abuse-Respect Age : Kochi, Kerala, is striving to become an Age-Friendly City as part of the WHO Global Network. The establishment of a resource center named Respect Age to combat elder abuse and ageism within the city. Respect Age will serve as a one-stop center for older persons, their families, and the public, providing support, information, and guidance. We operate a resource center that provides information, support, and referrals for elder abuse prevention and intervention. The center offers educational materials, counseling services, and legal assistance for older adults at risk of abuse.

Together, we empower communities, creating a world where inclusivity and wellbeing thrive.
