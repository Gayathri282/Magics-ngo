Our Services

At MAGICS, we offer a wide range of services to build inclusive, age-friendly communities and promote social wellbeing. From community health programs to innovative research and development, we are dedicated to driving positive change for all.

CEDAC

The Center for Elderly Development and Care (CEDAC) aims to enhance the quality of life for seniors through tailored programs and support services.

Learn More

VAYOPRATHIBHA

VAYOPRATHIBHA focuses on empowering older adults with skills and resources to engage actively in society, promoting their dignity and independence.

Learn More

SMRITHIYORAM

SMRITHIYORAM is dedicated to preserving the memories and experiences of seniors through storytelling and memory-sharing initiatives.

Learn More

VAYOSMITHAM

VAYOSMITHAM promotes holistic well-being for the elderly, integrating physical, mental, and emotional health in a supportive community setting.

Learn More

RESPECTAGE

RESPECTAGE advocates for the rights and dignity of older adults, ensuring they are treated with respect and compassion in all aspects of life.

Learn More

VIRTUAL REALITY ZONE

The VIRTUAL REALITY ZONE offers immersive experiences designed to engage seniors, enhancing their cognitive and emotional well-being through innovative technology.

Learn More

DEMENTIA FRIENDLY CARE

DEMENTIA FRIENDLY CARE provides specialized services and support for individuals with dementia, fostering an understanding and inclusive community.

Learn More

G-TOURISM

G-TOURISM promotes travel experiences tailored for seniors, encouraging exploration and adventure while ensuring accessibility and comfort.

Learn More

3G AGRICULTURE

3G AGRICULTURE focuses on sustainable farming practices that involve seniors, promoting community involvement and environmental stewardship.

Learn More

RESPIRATE WELLNESS

RESPIRATE WELLNESS aims to enhance respiratory health among seniors through education and wellness programs tailored to their needs.

Learn More

YUVAVIGYAN

YUVAVIGYAN fosters intergenerational learning and collaboration, encouraging youth and seniors to engage and learn from each other.

Learn More

AKSAM

AKSAM provides essential resources and support for aging individuals, promoting their health and well-being through comprehensive care services.
